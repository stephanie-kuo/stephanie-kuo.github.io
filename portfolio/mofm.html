What is Millions of Memories

Millions of Memories is an gaming application on tablet, it can help people better understand their families and create delightful family gathering time. This application can:

1) Help people record their life stories by audio recordings and pictures.
2) Let grandchildren have chances to know more about their grandparents.
3) Create fun interactions between family members.

Persona

Sometimes, we want to know more about our families' stories, but it is embarrassed to begin the talk. There are several roles involving in this situation.

Background

Lucky Kang (20, College Student)

Description

Lucy Kang is an undergraduate student. She goes back home to visit her parents once a month, and she meets her grandparents on holidays. Sometimes she feels embarassed to stay with her grandparents, because she knows little about her grandparents' lives. She is very curious about grandparents' life and their life story, but she has no idea how to begin to talk about the topics. Her grandfather has light Alzheimer's diseas, and she is also worried about that she may have less and less chances to know her grandfather's story. So she wants to do something to know more about her grandfather and keep those sotries in her memory.

Background

David Lee (72, Retired)

Description

David was working for an accounting firm for 40 years, and he has been retired for 10 years. He and his wife have 3 children and 5 grandchildren. His children and grandchildren visit him and his wife once a week, all of the family members gather together on holidays. He has light Alzheimer's disease, so sometimes he cannot remember all of his grandchildren and some old memories. He is self-esteem, and he rejects to admit that he has the disease. Somtimes he feels awkward in family gathering, because he cannot recognize everyone's name and face. He wants to know more about his family and hope them listen to him as well.

Background

Kelly Chang (45, housewife)

Description

Kelly has 2 sons and 1 daughter. All of her chilren are studying at schools. She is busy on house works and joining community activities. She and her husband visit their parents once a month. She is worried about parents' healthy situation, and she also wants her family members can make more efforts on supporting her parents. She thinks herself as a bridge between her parents and children. Sometimes she helps them communicate with each other and tell them how they are doing recently.

We want to make nostalgic feelings, so I use the texture of old paper as the main image and background. Paper is warm and contains lots of memories. And the paper texture can help elders feel less uncomfortable to use digital interface.

Another Possibility

Millions of Memories is based on the idea to have fun, so our team also tried to use gachapon as another interface. There is a question and a piece of puzzles at each capsule. Once you turn the key, one of the capsules will drop down. Users can open the capsule and ask one question, and the machine will start to record players' voice automatically. We hope to share happiness and cherish memories with every player in Millions of Memories through different kinds of interfaces.
